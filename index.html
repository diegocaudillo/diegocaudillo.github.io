Diego Caudillo, PhD

Diego Caudillo is an applied mathematician and data scientist with a decade of international experience spanning recommender systems, time series analysis, and machine learning on graph-structured data.
He holds a PhD in Mathematics from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU, 2025), where his research addressed the mathematical foundations of machine learning on graph-structured and sequential data. Before the doctorate, he worked as a data science consultant at C3 Consensus International, advising industrial, logistics, and public-sector clients across Mexico and the United States.
His academic formation includes an MSc in Probability and Statistics (CIMAT, Mexico) and a BSc in Mathematical Computing (University of Guanajuato), with early formation in competitive programming at the international stage, such as IOI, CIIC, and ACM-ICPC.
Diego speaks Spanish, English, Norwegian, and is learning French. He has worked in Norway, Canada, New Zealand, Germany, and Mexico.
His current focus is research-oriented roles in industry, at the intersection of applied mathematics, machine learning, and real-world complexity.
